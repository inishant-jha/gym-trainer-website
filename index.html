Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I train?

It depends on your goals and current fitness level. For beginners, I recommend 2-3 times per week. More advanced clients typically train 4-5 times weekly. We'll determine the optimal frequency during your initial assessment.

Do I need gym equipment at home?

Not necessarily. I can create effective bodyweight workouts that require minimal equipment. For more advanced training, some basic equipment like dumbbells or resistance bands is helpful but not required from day one.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients notice improvements in energy and strength within 2-3 weeks. Visible body changes typically appear after 4-6 weeks of consistent training and proper nutrition. Results vary based on individual factors and commitment.

Can you help with nutrition?

Yes! Nutrition is 70% of the results. I provide customized meal plans and nutritional guidance tailored to your goals, dietary preferences, and lifestyle. This is included in all premium training packages.
